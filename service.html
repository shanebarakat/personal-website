Service

Food Bank Volunteer - Kerr Street Mission

From 2019 till now, I play a vital role in supporting our community through a partnership with Cobbs Bread Bakery and Kerr Street Mission. My responsibilities include organizing unsold bread, packing it, and ensuring its timely delivery to Kerr Street Mission, a local food bank. This position helped me develop key skills such as logistical coordination, attention to detail, and time management. It also enhanced my community engagement by collaborating with local businesses and non-profits. Additionally, addressing unexpected challenges refined my problem-solving abilities and adaptability. This experience highlighted the importance of community involvement and social responsibility.
