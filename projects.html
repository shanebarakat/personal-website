Developer - HoloFund - BlockHack 2024

As a developer, I recently participated in Block Hack Toronto 2024, a hackathon hosted by George Brown College. My team and I created "HoloFund," a charity app designed to ensure donations reach their intended causes. Built using Flutter for the front end, Python and Firebase for the backend, and Astrakode for blockchain integration, HoloFund assigns credibility scores to charities, providing donors with transparency and confidence that their contributions are making a real impact. This project earned us 2nd place overall and 2nd in the "Astrakode Decentralized Charity Blockchain Challenge".

Skills: Flutter, Python, Firebase, Solodity

Developer - Spaces - StarterHacks 2024

At StarterHacks, I developed "Spaces," a dynamic web app designed to help students find unique spots on campus for various needs, whether it's studying, relaxing, or even taking a quick nap. "Spaces" leverages a user-friendly interface to enable students to discover and share hidden gems around their university. Users can post their favorite spots, complete with descriptions and photos, and explore locations recommended by their peers. They can also like and comment on others' posts, fostering a sense of community and engagement. The app aims to enhance the campus experience by making it easier for students to find the perfect environment for their current mood or task. By encouraging user-generated content and peer recommendations, "Spaces" promotes a more connected and resourceful student body, helping everyone make the most out of their campus spaces.

Skills: CSS, Javascript, Mongoose, MongoDB, HTML, Node.js, Express
